Notes on SC2 agents

agent - the person (or in this case AI) that is playing the game
observation - things you observe from the game
action - thing you decide to do at a specific time.
episode - one full training round, its usually from the start of the game til the end or when the reward is recieved
step - Observation -> Decision -> Action
reward - telling the ai that it did a good action as to improve that behavior
